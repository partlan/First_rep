Instructions on changing brake pads for hydraulic disc brakes on 
mountain or road bikes.


1. remove wheel
2. use a flat-head screwdriver to push the pistons back into the callipers
3. remove the pin holding the brakes pads in place
4. remove the old brake pads.
5. place new brake pads into the slot.
6. place pin through the calliper holes and through the pad holes.
7. place wheel back on.
8. adjust calliper position so that rubbing is minimal.
9. you may want to pump your brake handle about 20 times to resposition the 
pistons.
